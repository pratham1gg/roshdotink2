i've been meaning to start writing for a while now. not in any structured, "here's my content strategy" kind of way — just somewhere to put thoughts that are too long for a caption and too scattered for a conversation.

so here's a first post. it doesn't have a thesis. it's mostly just proof that this thing works.

---

lately i've been thinking about how weird it is that we call it a "feed". like, something is feeding you. you open an app and it just... delivers content into your mouth. you didn't go looking for it, it came to you, pre-selected, optimised.

i think that's part of why i wanted a corner of the internet that felt more deliberate. a place i actually had to sit down and make something, rather than consume something.

whether i'll keep writing here, i genuinely don't know. but it felt important to start.
